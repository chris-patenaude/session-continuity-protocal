# Support

## Where to get help

- **Questions & Clarifications:** Please use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/user/repo/discussions) (if enabled) or open an Issue with the `question` label.
- **Adoption Help:** If you are trying to implement SCP in your team and are stuck, feel free to open a discussion thread sharing your context.

## How to ask

When asking for help, please include:

1. Which version of SCP you are using.
2. Whether you are using the "Low-Tech," "Repo-Based," or "Agent-Based" workflow.
3. A snippet of your PMP or the specific situation causing friction.
